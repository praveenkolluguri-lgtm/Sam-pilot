Self Advocacy Mentor — pilot version. This is a small, closed pilot for AuDHD women. Please read and confirm each of the following before continuing.

What this tool does
SAM helps you reflect on and organise your experiences, and creates structured summaries that may support communication with healthcare professionals.

What it does not do
SAM does not provide diagnosis, medical advice, or clinical conclusions. It is not a replacement for clinical care.

Questions? Contact adhdgirlscommunication@gmail.com
